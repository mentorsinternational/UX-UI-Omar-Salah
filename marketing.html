Create a Contacts List

As a Mentor one of the most important tasks is staying in contact with ALL your Mentees. It is hard to do in the developing world. Even maintaining a Contact book becomes hard.
this is why we built a Contact List right in!

Create Your Own Text Reminders

How can you help keep track of your Mentees projects, and businesses it you cant keep track of your own obligations?
Create your own text reminders to help keep you on track and checking in.

Schedule Your Reminders

Text reminders are great, but why not be able to schedule them ahead of scheduled meetings, and appointments.
